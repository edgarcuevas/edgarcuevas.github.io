Hello!

My name is Edgar Cuevas. I am a proud Mexican-American software engineer and data scientist hoping to change the world one line of code at a time. Aside from programming, I love to read philosophy research papers and books on topics of morality, ethics, and metaphysics. In my free time, you can catch me hiking with my dog Scooby, while capturing some great nature shots using my Canon M50. I love working directly with non-profits (via volunteering and/or fundraising) to help social issues that are important to me. Feel free to reach out if you are interested in discussing programming and/or philosophy.
